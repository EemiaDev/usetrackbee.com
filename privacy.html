Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

TL;DR

The short version

  • We don't store your data. Job details go directly from your browser to your Google Sheet — nothing passes through our servers.
  • We only read pages when you ask. TrackBee reads the current tab only when you click Save. No background monitoring.
  • We never sell your data. Since we don't store it, there is nothing to sell.
  • No account required. We don't know your name, email, or identity.

TrackBee ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. We built this extension to solve a simple problem: helping job seekers track their applications with zero friction. This policy explains exactly what data we access, how it is used, and why you are always in control.

1. What We Access, And What We Don't

Permissions TrackBee uses:

  • Active Tab Reading: We only read the content of the current tab you are on, and only when our background script checks if the URL is a supported job site (to show the "+" badge), or when you click Save.
  • No Background Scraping: We do not track, read, or store your general browsing history at any time.
  • No Identity Tracking: TrackBee does not require an account. We do not know your name, email address, or personal identity.

2. How Your Data Is Used

The sole purpose of accessing job page data like Job Title, Company, and Compensation, is to transport it into your designated Google Sheet.

TrackBee has no central database. We do not intercept, store, or copy your job applications on any servers owned by us. Your data flows directly from your local browser to your Google Account via the official Google Sheets API.

3. Google API Services Disclosure

TrackBee's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

When you authenticate TrackBee with your Google Account, we request only the minimum permissions needed to append rows to the specific spreadsheet URL you provide. We cannot read your emails, and we do not have access to any other Google Drive files.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

Because we do not store your data, we have nothing to sell. TrackBee will never sell, rent, or share your job search history, preferences, or any other information with recruiters, advertisers, or third-party data brokers. Period.

5. Chrome Web Store Compliance

This privacy policy is provided to comply with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies. TrackBee is designed with a single purpose: to facilitate the rapid saving of job posting metadata into a user-owned Google Sheet.

Questions?

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@usetrackbee.com.
